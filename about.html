Our Story

Handcrafting furniture since 1985

Our Heritage

Founded in 1985 by master carpenter Rajiv Malhotra, Bharat Woods began as a small workshop in Jaipur, crafting custom furniture for local homes. What started as a passion project quickly grew into a respected name in fine furniture, known for our commitment to quality and traditional craftsmanship.

Today, while we've expanded our operations, we remain true to our roots. Each piece in our collection is still crafted with the same attention to detail and respect for the material that characterized our earliest works.

Our Craftsmanship

At Bharat Woods, we believe furniture should be built to last generations. That's why we:

  • Use only sustainably sourced hardwoods
  • Employ traditional joinery techniques
  • Finish each piece by hand
  • Subject every item to rigorous quality checks

Our team of over 50 skilled craftsmen combines decades of experience with a passion for their work, ensuring that each piece meets our exacting standards.

Our Values

Sustainability

We plant 10 trees for every one used in our furniture and use eco-friendly finishes.

Quality

Every piece is backed by our 10-year craftsmanship guarantee.

Community

We support local artisans and provide fair wages and benefits.
