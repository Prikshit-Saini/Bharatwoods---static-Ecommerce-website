About ElegantWood

Founded in 2023, ElegantWood brings together India's finest furniture craftsmen to create exceptional pieces for international markets.

Based in Jaipur, India, we combine traditional woodworking techniques with contemporary designs to create furniture that stands the test of time.

Our mission is to showcase India's rich woodworking heritage while meeting the highest international quality standards.
